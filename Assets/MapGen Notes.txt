===GLOBE VIEW==

1	Planet				40,960 km circumference
	-Global elevations
	-Global noise generation settings
	-Global max/min elevations
	-Global texture & heightmap
	
6	Quadrants			10,240 km quarter-circumference
	-Generates Regions for world view texturing and elevations
	-Holds region data and quadrant min/max
	
64	Regions		(8x8)	1,280 km edge (in cubed gameplay view)
	-Generates actual elevation and texture data to send to quadrants
	-Holds regional elevation data and regional min/max
	-Holds locales
	
256	Locales		(16x16)	80 km edge
	-Generated only when needed
	-Can be read for location properties
	-May be base for local gameplay map? (Too large!!)

===COORDINATES===

Coordinates have multiple conversions:
	-Longitude/Latitude (for UI and projection)
	-Local Position (XYZ) (from normalizing UVQ, for worldgen and math)
	-UVQ (for easy location data and texture maps)
		-Possible sub-uvq for per-locale coords
	-Root Coordinate is the corner of any particular level, not the center
